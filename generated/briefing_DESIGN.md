# DESIGN.md — Briefing Page (Vercel-leaning Mono Precision)

## Creative North Star
A calm, high-contrast product canvas for daily intelligence.
Not a magazine. Not a dashboard. Not a marketing landing page.
A precise reading surface that feels closer to Vercel than to a news portal.

## Tone
- monolithic but refined
- technical but not cold
- minimal but not empty
- confident, quiet, product-grade

## Core Principles

### 1. Vercel-like restraint
- avoid loud accent colors
- avoid decorative illustrations
- avoid theatrical asymmetry
- let spacing and typography carry the page

### 2. Mono-chromatic hierarchy
- background: deep black
- panels: slightly lifted charcoal
- text: near-white
- muted text: cool gray
- accent: one restrained technical green used sparingly

### 3. Strong type contrast
- headings: very large, tight, geometric sans
- labels: tiny uppercase mono
- body: clean, highly legible sans

### 4. Soft structure, not hard boxes
- rounded panels with thin low-contrast borders
- subtle section separation
- no heavy grid line gimmicks
- content should feel like it lives on one calm surface

## Typography
- Heading: Inter / bold / negative tracking
- Body: Inter / regular-medium
- Labels: JetBrains Mono / uppercase / wide tracking

## Layout
- centered hero, generous negative space
- hero summary first
- article cards below in a calm vertical rhythm
- right sidebar for meta and source index
- mobile first collapse without visual breakage

## Components

### Hero
- huge headline
- one short framing paragraph
- compact stats block on the right

### Article Card
- dark panel
- rounded corners
- soft border
- small mono kicker
- large headline
- concise summary / why it matters / insight sections

### Sidebar
- sticky on desktop
- source index + notes + exchange block
- low visual weight

## Motion
- subtle fade-up only
- soft hover lift on cards
- no flashy movement

## Avoid
- editorial newspaper styling
- harsh grid-line scaffolding
- multiple accent colors
- aggressive orange/green usage
- overly mechanical brutalism
- noisy backgrounds

## Implementation Goal
The page should feel like a premium product surface from a company like Vercel, adapted for briefing consumption.
